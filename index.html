Welcome!

I'm Álvaro, I'm 17 years old and I have created this website to present my research project for Coldplay. Currently, I'm studying at Pompeu Fabra High School.

Main idea of my project: to design and explain two new methods of obtaining sustainable energy. On the one hand, a giant ball that changes whose color depending on the pressure or blow it receives, to be thrown among the public and, on the other hand, an outsoule capable of adhering to any shoe and changing color on the force or pressure exerted by the feet.
